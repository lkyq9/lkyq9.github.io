Australian Shepherd

Australian Shepherds are otherwise known as Aussies! Despite their name Australian shepherds are not from Australia. They were bred in North America during the gold rush in the 1840’s as a work dog to help famers with livestock. Aussie’s or Australian shepherds still have their work dog attitude even today and are happiest when they have a job to do. Aussie’s can be outstanding companions if their intelligence and high energy are geared toward activities and dog sports.

Pros
  • Intelligent
  • Handles kids well
  • Dog friendly
  • Easy to train
  • Playful
  • Potential of being inexpensive


Cons
  • High Engergy
  • Does not do well in apartments
  • Potential for weight gain
  • Not for a novice dog owner
