Australian Shepherd

Australian Shepherds are otherwise known as Aussies! Despite their name Australian shepherds are not from Australia. They were bred in North America during the gold rush in the 1840’s as a work dog to help famers with livestock. Aussie’s or Australian shepherds still have their work dog attitude even today and are happiest when they have a job to do. Aussie’s can be outstanding companions if their intelligence and high energy are geared toward activities and dog sports.

Pros
  • Top 10 Intelligent breed
  • Intelligently cute funny
  • Handles kids well
  • Dog friendly
  • Easy to train
  • Playful
  • Potential of being inexpensive, as low as $300 without registration.
  • If you are allergic to dogs a longer haired dog like the Aussie is the way to go.
  • Can be left home alone for a significant amount of time without any destruction to the house according to the dog owners/breeders I have talked to during my dog searching process.
  • Comes in different sizes: toy, miniature, and Medium(regular).


Cons
  • High Engergy
  • Does not do well in apartments
  • Potential for weight gain
  • Not for a novice dog owner
  • Not "stupid" funny, according to the Youtube videos I have seen.
