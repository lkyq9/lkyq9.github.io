Shelters

A Shelter is where you can find a wide variety of dogs breeds who are different ages. Most of the dogs at a shelter are mutts. Even though most dogs at a shelter are mutts they can be just as loving and have a full personality. The pals you will find at at a shelter will also cost a lot less than one found at a breeder. So if you are on a budget maybe a shelter is another place to look. It depends on your view about it but, shelters cover the cost of getting the dogs neutered/ spayed before leaving the shelter. Below are some links to help you get started on this search. Hope you find your next buddy!
